Research Interests

Computational Neuroscience

Developing mathematical models to understand neural circuits and brain function. Focus on predictive coding and Bayesian inference in sensory processing.

Machine Learning in Neuroscience

Applying deep learning techniques to analyze neural data and develop brain-inspired algorithms for artificial intelligence systems.

Cognitive Neuroscience

Investigating the neural basis of decision-making, attention, and memory through computational modeling and experimental approaches.

My research bridges computational modeling with experimental neuroscience to advance our understanding of brain function and develop novel therapeutic approaches for neurological disorders.
